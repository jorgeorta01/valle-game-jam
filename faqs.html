Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions


What is a Game Jam?

A game jam is an event in which participants will make a game from scratch. Depending on the game format, participants will either work individually or in teams. Teams will generally consist of programmers, writers, artists and game designers. The game format is our focus which includes a theme, restriction and a time limit. The format will be announced one week before the event begins.
Please follow @vallegamedevs on Instagram for updates.


When and where is the Valle Game Jam?

Valle Game Jam will take place September 20th - 22, 2024 at Galaxy VR Video Game Lounge in Edinburg, Texas.


How do I enter the jam?

You can register with this link provided! (insert link here) There is no fee to participate in the event, however donations would be greatly appreciated so we can hold this event again in the future!
We recommend signing up by September 7th to ensure accommodations.


How will games be judged?

Games will be judged based on theme adherence, gameplay and sound design, and art design.

Questions About Participation


Who can participate?

This is an open event, but the prizes will be limited to students that are actively attending UTRGV.

How many participants are allowed per team?

The biggest team allowed is 4 members, however you are allowed to enter individually.

Will there be on-site computers? What should I bring to the jam?

Unfortunately, Valle Game Jam will not be providing on-site computers for participants to use. However, you are allowed to bring your own computers and other equipment you deem necessary.
